## What happened
Pet Poison Helpline published its 2025 annual report card, reviewing a year of toxicology consultations and cases in the United States.

## Key facts
- The report reflects 365 days of expert guidance to pet owners and veterinary professionals.
- It tracks exposures to common toxins reported through the helpline.
- The report is intended to help owners monitor and reduce potential risks.

## What pet owners should do
- Keep medications, foods, and household chemicals out of reach.
- Review the report card to understand common risks.
- Call Pet Poison Helpline or a veterinarian immediately if a pet may have ingested something toxic.
