## What happened
The Irish Mirror warned dog and cat owners to be wary of common Easter foods that are toxic to pets, such as chocolate, raisins, and sweets containing xylitol.

## Key facts
- Source: Irish Mirror
- Affected animals: dogs and cats
- Timing: Easter season
- These foods can cause acute poisoning, organ damage, or worse.

## What pet owners should do
Keep chocolate, sweets, grapes, raisins, and baked goods away from pets, educate guests, and contact a vet immediately if a pet ingests something dangerous.
