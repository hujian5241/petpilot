## What happened
farmersalmanac.com released a guide listing 30 common houseplants that are toxic to cats, dogs, and birds.

## Key facts
- Covers an A-to-Z range of popular indoor plants.
- Affected animals include cats, dogs, and birds.
- Many toxic plants may already be present in homes.

## What pet owners should do
- Identify the plants in your home and compare them to the guide.
- Remove or relocate toxic plants to areas pets cannot reach, and contact a vet if ingestion occurs.
