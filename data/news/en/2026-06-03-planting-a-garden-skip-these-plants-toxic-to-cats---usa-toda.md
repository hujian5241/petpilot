## What happened
USA Today published a report urging gardeners to skip plants that are toxic to cats.

## Key facts
- Affected animals: cats.
- Location: United States.
- Common garden plants can pose poisoning risks when cats chew leaves, petals, or stems.

## What pet owners should do
- Check plant toxicity before adding items to your garden.
- Create a cat-safe outdoor space or barrier off dangerous beds.
- Seek veterinary care immediately if ingestion is suspected.
