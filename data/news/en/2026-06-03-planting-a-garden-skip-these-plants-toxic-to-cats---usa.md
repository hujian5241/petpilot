## What happened
Google News surfaced a USA Today report warning cat owners about garden plants that can be toxic to felines during planting season.

## Key facts
- Affected animals: cats.
- Location: United States.
- The article highlights plants gardeners should avoid to keep cats safe.

## What pet owners should do
- Review garden plans and replace known toxic plants with cat-safe alternatives.
- Keep cats indoors or supervise them near garden beds.
- Contact a veterinarian if a cat shows signs of poisoning.
