## What happened
Catster published a vet-reviewed guide listing 16 everyday foods that are toxic to cats.

The report identifies common household foods that can harm cats, including items many owners may not realize are dangerous. Cats are the affected animals.
## What pet owners should do
Pet owners should review the list of toxic foods, keep those items out of reach, and contact a veterinarian immediately if a cat ingests something dangerous.
