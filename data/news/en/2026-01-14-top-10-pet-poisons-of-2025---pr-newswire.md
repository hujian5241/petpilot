## What happened
PR Newswire distributed a summary of the Pet Poison Helpline’s 2025 Top 10 Pet Poisons list, which details the most common poisoning hazards reported for pets during the year.

## Key facts
- The list identifies the most frequently reported substances involved in pet poisonings.
- Acetaminophen’s rise into the top 10 was a notable trend highlighted in related coverage.
- Data is based on U.S. cases from the Pet Poison Helpline.

## What pet owners should do
- Familiarize yourself with common pet toxins and store them securely.
- Monitor pets around medications, household cleaners, and foods.
- Call a veterinarian or poison helpline right away if exposure occurs.
