## What happened
PR Newswire shared Pet Poison Helpline’s “Top 10 Pet Poisons of 2025,” an annual ranking of the most common toxins reported to the helpline.

## Key facts
- The list helps pet owners and veterinarians recognize prevalent poisoning risks.
- Acetaminophen’s appearance among the top 10 indicates a notable shift in exposure patterns.

## What pet owners should do
- Audit your home for top-listed toxins, including medications, foods, and household products.
- Keep emergency numbers handy and call your vet or a poison helpline promptly if a pet is exposed.
