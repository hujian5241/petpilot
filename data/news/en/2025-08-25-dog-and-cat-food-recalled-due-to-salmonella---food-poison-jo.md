## What happened
Dog and cat food has been recalled due to potential Salmonella contamination, Food Poison Journal reports. The recall was announced as a pet-safety warning for owners of dogs and cats.

## Key facts
- Affected animals: dogs and cats
- Pathogen: Salmonella
- Product type: dog and cat food

## What pet owners should do
Check your pet food against the recall notice, stop feeding any affected product, and wash hands, bowls, and surfaces thoroughly. Watch pets for signs of Salmonella infection, such as vomiting, diarrhea, or lethargy, and contact a veterinarian if symptoms appear.
