Mars Petcare US, Inc. has recalled two lots of Pedigree Can High Protein Chopped Chicken & Duck Flavor wet dog food in 13.2-ounce cans. The recalled food may contain hard metal and plastic pieces that could injure dogs or cause choking, lacerations, or gastrointestinal blockages. The product had been sent to a third-party vendor for destruction but was fraudulently diverted and sold in the U.S. marketplace. The Hawaii Department of Health Food and Drug Branch cannot confirm whether the product was sold in Hawaii stores. There have been no reported injuries. Dog owners who purchased the product should check their labels and contact Pedigree Consumer Care at 800-525-5273 or visit the Pedigree website. Dogs that ate the food should be seen by a veterinarian.
