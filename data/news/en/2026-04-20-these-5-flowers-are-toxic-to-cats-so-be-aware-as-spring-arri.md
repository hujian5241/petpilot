## What happened
Daily Paws listed five flowers that are toxic to cats as spring arrives.

## Key facts
- The list includes lilies, tulips, daffodils, azaleas, and hyacinths.
- Lilies are especially dangerous and can cause kidney failure in cats.
- Affected species: cats.

## What pet owners should do
Avoid these flowers in bouquets and gardens, choose cat-safe alternatives such as roses, orchids, or snapdragons, and seek veterinary care immediately if a cat chews or ingests any petals, leaves, or bulbs.
