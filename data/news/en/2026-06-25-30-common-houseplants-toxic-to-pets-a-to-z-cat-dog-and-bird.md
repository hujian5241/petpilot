## What happened
Google News featured a Farmers' Almanac guide listing 30 common houseplants toxic to pets.

## Key facts
- Affected animals: dogs, cats, and birds.
- The guide covers popular houseplants from A to Z.
- Even common indoor plants can cause gastrointestinal, neurological, or organ damage.

## What pet owners should do
- Audit houseplants using a toxicity reference.
- Place hazardous plants out of reach or replace them with non-toxic species.
- Call a veterinarian or poison control if a pet nibbles a plant.
