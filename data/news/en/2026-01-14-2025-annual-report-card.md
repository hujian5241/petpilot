## What happened
Pet Poison Helpline published its 2025 Annual Report Card, recapping a year of veterinary toxicology consultations and poisoning cases across the United States.

## Key facts
- The report reflects 365 days of expert toxicology guidance and case data.
- It helps identify emerging and persistent pet poisoning risks.
- Notable coverage from DVM360 and PR Newswire emphasized the 2025 Top 10 Pet Poisons list.

## What pet owners should do
- Review the report to understand common household hazards.
- Keep medications, foods, and chemicals out of reach.
- Contact a veterinarian or poison helpline immediately if you suspect ingestion.
