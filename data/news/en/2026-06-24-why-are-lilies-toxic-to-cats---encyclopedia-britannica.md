## What happened
Encyclopedia Britannica published an explainer on why lilies are dangerous and potentially deadly to cats.

## Key facts
- Lilies are highly toxic to cats; even small amounts can cause kidney failure.
- All parts of the plant—including pollen, petals, leaves, and vase water—can be hazardous.
- Affected animals: cats.

## What pet owners should do
- Do not keep lilies in homes or gardens with cats.
- If a cat contacts or ingests any part of a lily, seek emergency veterinary care immediately.
