Go Raw LLC expanded a recall of its pet food products, including Steve's Real Food Chicken Recipe Freeze Dried, sold in more than 20 states including Texas. The recall affects both dog and cat food due to low levels of thiamine (Vitamin B1), which could lead to neurological issues in pets that consume the product. The recalled product is Steve's Real Food Chicken Recipe Freeze Dried in 1.25 lb bags, Lot C26022. The company had previously recalled three Quest Cat Food Chicken Recipe products. Pet owners should check their pet food for the recalled lot, stop feeding it, and contact a veterinarian if pets show neurological symptoms.

**Brand/Company:** Go Raw LLC

**Product:** Steve's Real Food Chicken Recipe Freeze Dried; Quest Cat Food Chicken Recipe Freeze Dried Nuggets; Quest Cat Food Chicken Recipe and Frozen Diet; Quest Cat Food Chicken Recipe Frozen Diet

**Affected animals:** dogs, cats

**Reason:** low levels of thiamine (Vitamin B1)

**Lot / UPC / Best by:** Steve's Real Food Chicken Recipe Freeze Dried in 1.25 lb bags, Lot C26022

**Location:** more than 20 states, including Texas
