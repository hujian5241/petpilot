Mars Petcare US Inc. of Tennessee has recalled two lots of Pedigree Can High Protein Chopped Chicken & Duck Flavor wet dog food. The recall affects 13.2-ounce cans with lot codes 613C3KKCFC and 613C1KKCFC, printed on the bottom of the can. The products may contain metal and plastic pieces. The company had identified the lots for destruction before distribution, but they were fraudulently diverted and sold by a third-party vendor in the U.S. marketplace. No sales information is available to confirm if the products were sold in Hawaii. No pet illnesses or injuries have been reported. Consumers who fed the recalled product to their dogs should contact a veterinarian. Mars can be reached at 1-800-525-5273 or pedigree.com/update for replacements.
