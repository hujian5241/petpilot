## What happened
A research study found that a wide range of dog and cat food sold in Japan contains PFAS, a group of industrial chemicals linked to health risks.

## Key facts
PFAS are persistent "forever chemicals" that can accumulate in animals. The study detected them in multiple pet-food products, affecting both dogs and cats.

## What pet owners should do
Monitor brand and product news, discuss your pet's diet with a veterinarian, and consider switching to products with lower contamination when verified information becomes available.
