## What happened
A dog treat product was recalled due to possible Salmonella contamination, according to Food Poison Journal.

## Key facts
- **Source:** Food Poison Journal (March 1, 2026)
- **Affected animals:** Dogs
- **Pathogen:** Salmonella
- **Risk:** Salmonella can cause gastrointestinal illness in dogs and may spread to humans.

## What pet owners should do
- Stop feeding the recalled treats.
- Check packaging and lot numbers against the recall notice.
- Wash hands and surfaces after handling pet food.
- Contact a veterinarian if your dog shows vomiting, diarrhea, fever, or lethargy.
