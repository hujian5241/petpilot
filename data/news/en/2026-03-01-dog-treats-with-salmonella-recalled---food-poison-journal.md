## What happened
Food Poison Journal reported that dog treats were recalled due to Salmonella contamination.

## Key facts
- Date: March 1, 2026
- Affected animals: dogs
- Pathogen: Salmonella
- Source: Food Poison Journal

## What pet owners should do
Check treat packages against the recall notice, stop feeding affected products, and monitor dogs for vomiting, diarrhea, fever, or lethargy. Contact a veterinarian if symptoms appear. Wash hands after handling pet food.
