## What happened
DVM360 reported on the Pet Poison Helpline’s 2025 findings, highlighting that acetaminophen has risen into the top 10 list of pet poisons for the year.

## Key facts
- Acetaminophen, a common over-the-counter pain reliever, is toxic to pets.
- The article reflects U.S. poison-case trends from 2025.
- Pet Poison Helpline’s annual report card informed the coverage.

## What pet owners should do
- Never give pets acetaminophen or other human medications without veterinary guidance.
- Store all medicines in pet-proof containers.
- Seek immediate veterinary care if ingestion is suspected.
