Mars Petcare US, Inc. has issued a voluntary recall for two lots of Pedigree High Protein Chopped Chicken & Duck Flavor wet dog food in the United States. The affected 13.2-ounce cans, with lot codes 613C3KKCFC and 613C1KKCFC, may contain sharp pieces of metal and plastic. The products failed quality control standards and had been sent to a third-party vendor for destruction, but were mistakenly diverted and sold in retail stores. Consuming the food could cause dogs to choke, suffer cuts to the mouth or digestive tract, or experience intestinal blockages. Pet owners should not feed the recalled food to their dogs and should contact Pedigree Consumer Care at 1-800-525-5273 for a replacement or assistance. As of July 2, no illnesses or injuries had been reported.
