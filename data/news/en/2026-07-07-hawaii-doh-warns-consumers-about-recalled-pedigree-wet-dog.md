The Hawaiʻi Department of Health warned pet owners about a recall of Pedigree High Protein Chopped Chicken & Duck Flavor wet dog food after manufacturer Mars Petcare said two lots may contain metal and plastic fragments. The 13.2-ounce cans carry lot codes 613C3KKCFC and 613C1KKCFC on the bottom. The affected lots were slated for destruction but were allegedly diverted and sold through unauthorized U.S. channels, so it is unclear whether they reached Hawaiʻi. No pet illnesses or injuries have been reported. Owners should check their cans, stop feeding the recalled food to dogs, and consult a veterinarian if their dog consumed it. Replacements can be requested by calling Pedigree Consumer Care at 800-525-5273 from 3 a.m. to 3 p.m. Monday to Friday.
