## What happened
PetfoodIndustry reported that RCL Foods recalled pet food because of potential Salmonella contamination.

## Key facts
- Date: March 6, 2026
- Affected animals: pets (other)
- Pathogen: Salmonella
- Source: PetfoodIndustry

## What pet owners should do
Compare purchased pet food against the recall details, discontinue feeding affected products, and watch for signs of illness. Seek veterinary care if symptoms develop. Clean bowls and surfaces after handling food.
