## What happened
Mamavation published a guide to help dog owners choose natural, organic, and non-toxic dog food, emphasizing the need to avoid harmful chemicals in commercial pet diets.

## Key facts
- Source: Mamavation
- Affected animals: dogs
- Focus: safer, lower-contaminant dog food options
- Highlights concerns about hidden toxins in pet food ingredients.

## What pet owners should do
Look for certified organic or transparency-focused brands, avoid artificial additives and known contaminants, and consult a veterinarian when switching diets.
