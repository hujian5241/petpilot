Mars Petcare has recalled two lots of 13.2-ounce Pedigree Can High Protein Chopped Chicken & Duck Flavor wet dog food. The cans failed quality checks and were sent to be destroyed, but were fraudulently diverted and sold nationwide. The company warned that metal and plastic parts in the food could cause choking, cuts, or gastrointestinal blockages in dogs. No injuries or deaths have been reported. Customers should stop feeding the affected food immediately and contact the company for a replacement; if a pet may have eaten the food, owners should contact a veterinarian.
