## What happened
Catster published a vet-reviewed guide listing 16 everyday foods that are toxic to cats.

## Key facts
Common human foods—including onions, garlic, chocolate, caffeine, alcohol, grapes, xylitol, and raw dough—can cause anemia, seizures, kidney failure, or gastrointestinal upset. Even small amounts may be dangerous.

## What pet owners should do
Keep these foods out of reach, avoid feeding table scraps, and seek immediate veterinary care if ingestion is suspected.
