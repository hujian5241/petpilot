## What happened
Farmers’ Almanac published a guide on June 25, 2026, listing 30 common houseplants that are toxic to pets, including cats, dogs, and birds.

## Key facts
- Affected animals: cats, dogs, and birds
- Format: A-to-Z guide covering 30 common houseplants
- Includes both popular decorative and easy-care indoor plants
- Source: farmersalmanac.com, published June 25, 2026

## What pet owners should do
Audit houseplants against the guide, relocate toxic species out of reach, and choose pet-safe alternatives such as spider plants, Boston ferns, or cat grass. Contact a veterinarian or poison helpline if a pet ingests a suspect plant.
