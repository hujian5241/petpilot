## What happened
USA Today published a gardening safety report on June 3, 2026, warning U.S. cat owners to avoid planting common garden species that are toxic to felines.

## Key facts
- Affected animals: cats
- Focus: garden and outdoor plants
- Location: United States
- Source: USA Today, published June 3, 2026

## What pet owners should do
Before planting, check whether a plant is toxic to cats using the ASPCA or another trusted animal-poison reference. Keep cats indoors or supervised in gardens, and remove any known toxic specimens from yards and planters.
