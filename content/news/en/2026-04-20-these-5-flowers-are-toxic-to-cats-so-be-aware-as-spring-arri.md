## What happened
Daily Paws highlighted five common flowers that are toxic to cats as spring arrives.

## Key facts
Lilies, tulips, daffodils, azaleas, and hyacinths can cause symptoms ranging from mouth irritation and vomiting to kidney failure or cardiac effects, depending on the plant and amount consumed.

## What pet owners should do
Choose pet-safe plants, display bouquets out of reach, and act quickly if a cat shows signs of poisoning.
