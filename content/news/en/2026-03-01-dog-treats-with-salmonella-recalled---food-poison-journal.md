## What happened
A recall was announced for dog treats potentially contaminated with Salmonella, according to a March 1 report by Food Poison Journal.

## Key facts
- Pathogen: Salmonella
- Affected pets: dogs
- Product type: dog treats
- Source date: March 1, 2026

## What pet owners should do
Stop feeding the recalled treats; wash hands, bowls, and surfaces; watch for vomiting, diarrhea, or lethargy; and contact a veterinarian if symptoms appear.
