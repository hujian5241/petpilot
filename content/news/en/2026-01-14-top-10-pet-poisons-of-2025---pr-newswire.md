## What happened
PR Newswire distributed a report on the top 10 pet poisons of 2025, drawing from the Pet Poison Helpline's annual data.
## Key facts
- The top-10 list highlights the most common toxins reported to the helpline.
- Acetaminophen is a notable new addition.
- The information is intended for pet owners and veterinary professionals in the United States.
## What pet owners should do
- Keep the top-10 list handy as a reference for common household dangers.
- Secure all medications, foods, and chemicals away from pets.
- Contact a veterinarian or poison helpline immediately after any suspected ingestion.
