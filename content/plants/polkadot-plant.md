# Polka Dot Plant

Polka dot plants are compact, colorful houseplants with leaves spotted in pink, red, or white. They are non-toxic to dogs and cats, making them a fun and safe way to add color to a pet-friendly home.

They prefer bright, indirect light and high humidity. Polka dot plants are a great pet-safe alternative to toxic colorful plants like coleus or croton.
