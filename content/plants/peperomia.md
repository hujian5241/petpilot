# Peperomia

Peperomias are a diverse group of small, succulent-like houseplants with thick, fleshy leaves. They come in many shapes, sizes, and colors and are non-toxic to dogs and cats, making them ideal for pet-friendly homes.

Because they stay small and tolerate a range of light conditions, peperomias are great for desks, shelves, and terrariums. They are a safe alternative to toxic succulents such as jade or aloe vera.
