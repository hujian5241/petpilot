# Sesame Seeds

Plain sesame seeds are generally safe for dogs in very small amounts. They add a little crunch and healthy fat but are not a dietary necessity.

Tahini and sesame-based products may contain added salt, sugar, or garlic, which should be avoided. Cats should only have a trace amount.
