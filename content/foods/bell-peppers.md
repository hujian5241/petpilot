# Bell Peppers

Bell peppers, especially red ones, are safe for dogs and provide vitamin C and antioxidants. They are crunchy and low in calories. Remove the stem and seeds before feeding, as they can be difficult to digest.

Cats may not be interested in bell peppers but can have a tiny piece. Never confuse bell peppers with hot peppers or chili peppers, which can cause stomach pain and vomiting. Feed peppers plain without oils or seasonings.
