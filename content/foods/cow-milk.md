# Cow's Milk

Cow's milk is not toxic to dogs or cats, but most adult pets are lactose intolerant. Drinking milk can cause diarrhea, gas, bloating, and vomiting. Some pets tolerate small amounts better than others, but milk is unnecessary in a balanced pet diet.

If you want to share a milk-like treat, choose lactose-free milk or a pet-specific milk replacer. Puppies and kittens should never be fed cow's milk as a substitute for mother's milk or proper veterinary-formulated formula.
