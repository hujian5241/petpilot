# Almond Milk

Plain, unsweetened almond milk is a safe occasional treat for pets who tolerate it. It is lactose-free and lower in calories than dairy milk. However, flavored or sweetened versions may contain xylitol, which is extremely toxic to dogs, or chocolate and vanilla extracts that add risk. Always check the ingredient list before sharing any plant milk.
