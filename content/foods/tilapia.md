# Tilapia

Tilapia is a mild, low-fat whitefish that is generally well tolerated by dogs and cats. It can be a good option for pets with sensitive stomachs or those needing a lean protein.

Cook tilapia plainly and remove all bones. Avoid fried, breaded, or seasoned fish.
