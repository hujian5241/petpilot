  dogs:
    status: toxic
    severity: high
    summary: >-
      Liquid termiticides and sprays can contain neurotoxic insecticides; dogs
      must be kept away from treated areas until products are completely dry and
      any fumes have dissipated.
  cats:
    status: toxic
    severity: high
    summary: >-
      Cats are highly sensitive to many termiticides, especially pyrethroids and
      fipronil; they must be excluded from treated areas until products are
      fully dry and well ventilated.
