# Tulip

Tulips are beloved spring bulbs known for their bold, cup-shaped flowers. The highest concentration of toxins is in the bulb, but leaves and flowers can also cause problems if chewed. Pets may experience drooling, vomiting, diarrhea, and depression.

Dogs are especially at risk if they dig up and eat bulbs from pots or garden beds. Store potted tulips out of reach and discard any chewed plant material promptly. Consider pet-safe flowering plants for indoor color instead.
