# Sunflower

Sunflowers are tall, cheerful annuals with large seed heads. The flowers, leaves, and seeds are safe for pets. Unsalted sunflower seeds are a popular dog treat.

Avoid seeds with salt, seasonings, or chocolate coatings. Sunflower stalks can be fibrous and hard to digest, so limit chewing on thick stems.
