# Parlor Palm

Parlor palms are compact, slow-growing palms that have been popular houseplants since Victorian times. They tolerate low light and neglect better than many tropical plants and are completely non-toxic to dogs and cats.

Their graceful, arching fronds make them ideal for tabletops or corners. Parlor palms are an excellent pet-safe alternative to toxic palms and larger foliage plants.
