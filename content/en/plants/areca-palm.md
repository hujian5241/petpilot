# Areca Palm

Areca palms are elegant, clumping palms with feathery, arching fronds. They are non-toxic to both dogs and cats and can add a tropical feel to a home without the risks associated with true palms such as sago palm.

These palms prefer bright, indirect light and consistent moisture. Because they are safe, areca palms are a great choice for households that want a large statement plant without worrying about pet poisoning.
