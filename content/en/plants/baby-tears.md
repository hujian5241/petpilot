# Baby Tears

Baby tears is a delicate, creeping plant with tiny round leaves that forms a lush mat of green. It is non-toxic to dogs and cats and is often used in terrariums, hanging baskets, or as ground cover.

It prefers bright, indirect light and consistently moist soil. Baby tears is a safe, low-growing alternative to toxic trailers like pothos or English ivy.
