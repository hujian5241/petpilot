# Chicken Noodle Soup

Chicken noodle soup can be comforting, but commercial versions are loaded with sodium and frequently contain onion and garlic powder. Homemade soup made without salt, onion, or garlic can be safe in small amounts. The noodles themselves are not harmful, but they offer little nutrition. If your pet slurps a bowl of regular soup, a vet call is wise because of the salt and alliums.
