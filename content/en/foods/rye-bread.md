# Rye Bread

Plain rye bread without toxic additives is generally safe for dogs in small amounts. Rye bread is denser than wheat bread and can be harder to digest, so keep portions small. Some rye breads contain caraway seeds, which can cause mild stomach upset in large quantities.

Raisin rye bread is dangerous and should never be fed to pets. As with all breads, never feed raw dough. Cats should receive only a crumb.
