# Skim Milk

Skim milk has less fat than whole milk but still contains lactose, which most adult dogs and cats cannot digest well. It can cause diarrhea, gas, and abdominal discomfort. Skim milk is not a necessary or beneficial treat for pets.

If you choose to give a milk-based treat, lactose-free milk or a small amount of plain yogurt is usually better tolerated. Always prioritize fresh water for hydration.
