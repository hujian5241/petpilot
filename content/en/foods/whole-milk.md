# Whole Milk

Whole milk is high in fat and lactose, making it a poor choice for most pets. Dogs that drink whole milk are likely to develop diarrhea, gas, and stomach cramps. The high fat content can also increase the risk of pancreatitis in sensitive dogs.

Cats do not need milk after weaning and usually become lactose intolerant. Whole milk should not be used as a treat or meal replacement. Water is the best drink for pets.
