# Rutabaga

Rutabaga is a root vegetable that is safe for dogs when cooked and served plain. It is a good source of fiber and vitamin C. Raw rutabaga is too hard for most pets to chew and digest properly.

Cats can eat a very small amount of cooked rutabaga. Avoid rutabagas prepared with butter, salt, garlic, onions, or heavy seasonings.
