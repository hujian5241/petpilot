# Cayenne Pepper

Cayenne pepper and other chili powders contain capsaicin, which causes burning sensations. Pets are not accustomed to spicy food and may drool, vomit, or paw at their mouths after contact. Chili powder blends may also contain garlic and onion powder, making them even more hazardous. Keep spicy dishes out of reach.
