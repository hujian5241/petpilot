# Chai Latte

Chai lattes contain brewed black tea with caffeine, spices such as cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, and nutmeg, plus milk and sugar. The caffeine alone is enough to make this drink unsafe for pets, and nutmeg can add neurotoxicity. A small accidental lick may only cause mild stomach upset, but larger amounts require emergency evaluation.
